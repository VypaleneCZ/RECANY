# Game Development Structure for RECANY

## Overview
RECANY is designed as a hybrid RPG-strategy game set in a rich, immersive environment based on medieval Bohemia, specifically focusing on the Řeřany to Pardubice campaign. The game incorporates a complex building system, dynamic terrain generation, defense mechanics, and an innovative combat system that combines RPG and strategy elements.

---

## Core Components

### 1. Building System
- **Resource Management**: Players must gather resources (wood, stone, metal) to create structures.
- **Construction Mechanics**: Utilize a modular building system that allows for customization of buildings (e.g., houses, fortifications, workshops).
- **Upgrades**: Buildings can be upgraded for improved functionality and defense.

### 2. Terrain Generation
- **Procedural Generation**: Use algorithms to create a realistic representation of medieval Bohemia.
  - **Biome Diversity**: Include different types of terrains such as forests, hills, rivers, and towns.
  - **Dynamic Weather**: Implement weather systems that affect gameplay and strategy.
- **Cultural Landmarks**: Incorporate historical landmarks to enrich the game experience.

### 3. Defense Mechanics
- **Base Defense Strategies**: Players can design defensive structures and traps.
- **NPC Defenders**: Hire and train NPCs to protect the player's territory.
- **Dynamic Events**: Introduce random events like bandit raids that require strategic defense planning.

### 4. Combat System
- **Hybrid Combat Mechanics**: Blend RPG elements (character skills, leveling) with strategy (unit placement, tactics).
  - **Character Classes**: Define various classes (warrior, archer, mage) with unique abilities.
  - **Tactical Control**: Allow players to command units in real-time or through strategic pause.
- **Unit Types**: Introduce a variety of units with different strengths, weaknesses, and roles. 
- **Skill Trees**: Develop skill trees that enhance character abilities and strategies based on player choices.

## Campaign Focus: Řeřany to Pardubice
- **Narrative**: Craft a compelling storyline that guides players through the campaign.
- **Quests**: Develop a range of quests (main and side missions) that advance the plot and uncover the history of Bohemia.
- **Faction System**: Introduce factions that players can ally with or oppose, affecting diplomacy and strategy.

## Conclusion
The RECANY game aims to provide a deep, strategic, and engaging experience that encapsulates both the historical and fantastical elements of medieval life in Bohemia. Players will not only manage resources and combat but also engage in a rich narrative woven through the landscape of Řeřany to Pardubice.
